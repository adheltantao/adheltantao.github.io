Full agency architecture

Nav, hero with client logos, stats bar, services with hover animation, case studies, team section, testimonials, and split contact section - this is the complete structure of a real agency homepage, not a simplified demo.

Light theme with dark hero

Mixed theme page - dark hero for impact, light body for readability, dark contact section to close. This three-zone approach is a common pattern for agency sites and shows ability to handle complex multi-theme layouts.

CSS hover animations

Service cards use a CSS scaleY transform on a left border strip that animates on hover - no JavaScript. Team cards and testimonials lift with translateY. All transitions are CSS-only for performance.

Teal accent palette

Fourth distinct color palette across the portfolio pages - demonstrating that every niche gets appropriate design language, not one template recolored. Agency sites typically use professional, trust-signaling colors.
