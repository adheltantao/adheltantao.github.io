Am I Eligible?

Eligibility Pathways

Italian citizenship passes through bloodlines. If any of the following apply, you likely qualify.

You May Qualify If...

  • You have an Italian-born grandparent or great-grandparent
  • Your Italian ancestor never naturalized before your parent was born
  • You have Italian citizenship through a spouse (marriage pathway)
  • Your Italian ancestor emigrated after 1861
  • You have documented lineage through the male or female line

Benefits of Italian Citizenship

  • EU passport - live and work in 27 European countries
  • Access to Italian national healthcare and education
  • Pass citizenship to your children and grandchildren
  • No renunciation of your current citizenship required
  • Italian property ownership rights and tax benefits
