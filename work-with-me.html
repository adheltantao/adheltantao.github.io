How I Work

No hand-holding required

You won't get a junior figuring things out on your dime. Here's roughly how engagements actually go.

01
Scope it in one conversation
Tell me the problem, not the solution. I'll tell you straight if it's a quick fix, a build, or something that needs more clarity before we start — no padding the scope to look busy.
02
I work async, you stay in the loop
PHT timezone, but built around minimal back-and-forth — clear status updates, no chasing me for progress.
03
Ship, then tighten
First pass gets it live and functional. Then we refine based on what's actually performing — copy, layout, tracking, whatever the data points to.
04
Stay or step back, your call
Some projects are one-and-done. Others turn into ongoing retainers once the trust is there. No pressure either way.
