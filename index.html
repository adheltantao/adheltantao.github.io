About Adhel Tantao

I build WordPress and Elementor pages that are designed to perform - not just look good.

6+ years building landing pages, sales funnels, and content sites for US and AU digital marketing agencies and founders. I write custom CSS when builders hit their limits, implement proper on-page SEO structure from the ground up, and configure tracking so nothing falls through the cracks.

My background started in SEO content writing for affiliate sites - meaning I understand how pages need to be built to rank and convert, not just how they look. From there I moved into landing page production for paid media campaigns across insurance, crypto, e-commerce, and legal services niches.

Long-term roles at Search District (AU) and UrbanityMedia (US) gave me real agency workflow experience: site overhauls, algorithm recovery, Screaming Frog audits, and full-cycle WordPress operations at scale.

Background

Experience & Specialties

WordPress & Elementor Development -

Built and customized hundreds of marketing landers and sales funnels using WordPress and Elementor across insurance, crypto, e-commerce, and legal services niches. Comfortable extending Elementor with custom HTML/CSS - keeping code clean and load times fast.

Also experienced with Divi, Thrive Architect, WPBakery, Beaver Builder, Gutenberg, and ACF. Outside WordPress, proficient in LanderLab. Full-cycle WordPress operations including plugin configuration, hosting setup, FTP-level backend work, and multi-site deployment.

SEO & Content Optimization +

Approaches SEO from the builder's perspective - pages are structurally sound from the start. Proper heading hierarchy, metadata, internal linking, schema-ready markup, and image optimization are defaults, not afterthoughts.

Led full site overhaul on CryptoHead.io following the Google Helpful Content Update - Screaming Frog crawl audits, internal link restructuring, content refreshes, and metadata cleanup at scale. Experienced with ClearScope, SurferSEO, OutRanking, and Ahrefs.

Paid Media Operations (Facebook, TikTok & Snapchat) +

Solid operational experience on the paid media side - compliant campaign infrastructure, Multilogin and proxy setups, landing page and tracking configuration, and campaign monitoring across insurance, crypto, and e-commerce verticals.

Handles the full operational checklist: account infrastructure, proxy configuration, page warming, ad set builds, creative uploads, comment moderation, and optimization loops. Works best as the execution layer behind a strategy team.

Tracking & Technical Integration +

End-to-end tracking configurations: UTM parameters, ad network pixels, custom scripts, Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity, and Meta Pixel. Proficient with Voluum and Redtrack for campaign-level tracking. Comfortable with privacy environments via Multilogin and AdsPower.

Content Writing & Copywriting +

Background in content writing - product reviews, blog posts, ghostwriting, and SEO articles across Amazon affiliate, crypto, and lifestyle niches. Writes clean, readable English and understands how to structure content for both search intent and human readability.
