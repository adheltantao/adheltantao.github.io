How I Work

My writing process

Developed across hundreds of articles for real clients with real deadlines.

1
Understand the brief and keywords
I work from whatever you provide — target keywords, angle, word count, audience. If you want me to handle keyword research using Ahrefs or SurferSEO, I can do that too.
2
Research the topic properly
I check the top-ranking pages, pull relevant data, and build a clear outline before writing a single word. For specialist niches, I ask questions rather than guess.
3
Write with SEO baked in
Proper heading hierarchy, natural keyword use, internal linking suggestions, and optimized meta where applicable. Not stuffed — structured correctly.
4
Edit and optimize
Grammarly pass, readability check, and if we're using ClearScope or SurferSEO, I'll optimize against the target score before submitting.
5
Deliver on time, publish if needed
Average turnaround for a 2,000–3,000 word article is 1–2 days. I can also publish directly in WordPress if you'd prefer to hand off the whole thing.
