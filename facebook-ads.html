Straight Answers

Common questions from past clients

Pulled directly from real screening conversations — kept as they were answered.

How many years of Facebook Ads experience do you have?
6 years — managed eCommerce, crypto, and insurance lead campaigns across multiple ad accounts.
Have you worked with e-commerce brands before?
Yes — I ran my own dropshipping store (both standalone and on Shopify) before moving into agency-side media buying, which gave me both the buyer and seller perspective on what actually converts.
What's the highest daily budget you've handled?
Around $2,000+/day on Facebook and $500+/day on TikTok, across active conversion campaigns.
What is a Facebook Pixel, in your own words?
A small piece of code placed on the website that tracks user behavior — page visits, add-to-carts — to measure ad performance, optimize targeting, and power retargeting to people who already visited the site.
How have you used AI tools like Claude in real campaign work?
Used Claude to help build interactive landers for ad campaigns — the most effective was a chatbot-style lander. Also used it to help test and optimize campaigns through a funnel-based approach.
